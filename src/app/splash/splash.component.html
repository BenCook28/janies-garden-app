Welcome to Janie's Garden App!

Create, View, Edit and Delete Plants in Your Garden

Use this website to track all of the plants in your garden.

For each plant, enter information you want to track - such as common name, water requirements, and location on your property.

View your entire garden, shown as a list of the plants you've entered.

Edit and delete plants as needed.

Then, get back to gardening!



The inspiration for this web application comes from the co-creator's mother, who loves digging in the earth.
